Who I Am

I grew up in Kuna, Idaho and later moved to Wilsonville, Oregon, where sports and competition—especially wrestling—taught me discipline and grit.

After high school, I started my sales career in Orange County, California as a door-to-door representative for Dewey Pest Control. That experience launched me into direct sales and gave me a taste for performance-based work.

I then spent a year at BYU–Hawaii, and later managed sales teams in Chico, California and Dallas, Texas. Those roles taught me how to lead people, coach performance, and think in terms of revenue and systems, not just individual effort.

In between, I served a mission in Villahermosa, Tabasco, Mexico, which shaped my testimony, leadership style, and Spanish fluency. Now I’m at BYU in Provo, focused on finance and real estate while building a life centered on faith, family, and service.
